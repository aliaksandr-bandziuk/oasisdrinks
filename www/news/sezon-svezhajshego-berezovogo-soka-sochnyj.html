Season of the freshest birch sap "Juicy"

Despite the cool April weather, birch sap harvesting is in full swing.

Juice carefully collected in Belarusian forestries is delivered to our plant, where it is filtered and purified from all kinds of impurities and bottled in packaging from renewable sources of raw materials.

All manufactured products are labeled with the “Natural Product” sign. It is applied only to products made from natural food raw materials without GMOs, dyes and preservatives.

From day to day, the freshest birch sap "Juicy" will be in the shops of every city in Belarus. Traditionally, the beloved birch sap "Juicy" is bottled in a liter package, a two-liter family format and a 200 ml portion package.
