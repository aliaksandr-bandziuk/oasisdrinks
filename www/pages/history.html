The company's development key stages

March 2006 г.

Detroit Belarus Juice Company (DBJC) was founded

OAO 'Bobruisk cannery' was acquired

we have approved a plan for the enterprise reconstruction

the ‘Staraya Krepost’ factory official opening ceremony under the participation of the Prime Minister of Belarus Sergei Sidorsky

May 2008 г.

the first batch of ‘Sochniy’ juice was shipped

August 2008 г.

the first million liters of juice was sold

October 2008 г.

Prigat juices start of bottling - the first licensed foreign juice brand in the Republic of Belarus

December 2008 г.

‘Sochniy’ juice became the sales market leader among all juice brands in Belarus

‘Staraya Krepost’ became the winner of the competition "The Best Entrepreneur of 2008" of the Mogilev region in the nomination of food producers

May 2009 г.

ISO 9001 international certificate was received, ‘Staraya Krepost’ became the leader in terms of juice sales on the Belarusian market

August 2009 г.

‘Staraya Krepost’ company got ‘The Green Certificate’

October 2009 г.

a boy Vladik from the advertisement of ‘Sochniy’ was awarded the title of favorite advertising hero in Belarus

production under the new brand ‘Exotic’ has kicked off

juice-containing carbonated drinks "Exotic Bubbles" sales kick off

OeTTINGER - No.1 Germany’s licensed beer production was launched
