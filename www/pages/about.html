About us

The Oasis is the No.1 producer of juices and nectars in Belarus.

The main activities of the company are the production of baby food, juices and nectars, as well as beer

The Oasis is an international holding with beverage factories operating in Belarus, Russia and Kazakhstan.

In Belarus, we own a plant for the production of juices, nectars, as well as juices and nectars for baby food. The plant was established in 2008 in Bobruisk, Mogilev region. The enterprise is equipped with Tetra Pak high-tech equipment, which allows the production of modern high-quality products.

No.1 producer of juices in Belarus

We implement all state-of-the-art international standards. We are an international holding operating factories in Belarus, Russia and Kazakhstan.

Key facts about 'Oasis' are:

- No.1 company in the juice market for over 14 years. Our main strategic goal is to become a key player in the beverage market;

- we own the 'Sochniy' is No.1 brand among juices and nectars market, apart from that our brands are 'Villa Dini', 'Bambolina' and 'Juicy Snack';

- the largest and most modern juice production in Belarus;

- we are awarded signs of honor for outstanding achievements in various fields.

We work out to ensure that you get an opportunity to buy the best products in our retail stores.
We strive to create and offer our consumers the most delicious and high-quality drinks in a wide range of categories.
